Lifestyle


Eating Out In Budapest

Budapest has an amazing array of restaurants catering to both local and international tastes. Those looking for global flavors will be spoilt in choice, as the city offers a variety of international restaurants including, Mexican, Italian, Indian and Chinese. Those looking for something local can sample authentic Hungarian cousin, which is famed for its stews and pastries.

Shopping In Budapest

For the shopaholic who wants a bit of everything, the malls in Budapest have a lot to offer. Some of the most popular malls include the WestEnd City Center, Corvin Plaza and the Mammut Shopping and Entertainment Center, where a combination of international brands and local shops can be found.

Night Life In Budapest

Hungarian wine is highly underrated and, aside from the well-known Tokaji, the country boasts a number of great wines. Budapest offers a variety of wine bars in which you can sample the country's fine wines in beautiful surroundings, such as the area around the famous St Stephen's Basilica. Each bar has its own distinct personality, with an electric mix of art and design, and diverse music and décor.
